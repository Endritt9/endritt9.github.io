30 Days Return

At Hasani Shop, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your football boots, we offer a hassle-free 30 days return policy.

What Qualifies for a Return:

  • Unused and in the same condition as received
  • In the original packaging
  • With all tags and labels attached
  • Not damaged or altered in any way

How to Initiate a Return:

  1. Contact our Customer Support:
    • Email: return@yourwebsite.com
    • Phone: (+3XX) 04x XXX-XXXX
  2. Provide Order Details: Please have your order number and details ready when contacting us. This will help us process your return quickly.
  3. Pack the Items: Securely pack the football boots in their original packaging.
  4. Shipping: Ship the return package to the address provided by our customer support team.

Return Conditions:

  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to an error on our part or a defective product.

Refund Process:

  • Once we receive your return and verify the conditions, we will process your refund.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Exchanges:

If you need a different size or color, please initiate a return for the unwanted item and place a new order.
